Cascadia AI Collective is a safe and supportive ecosystem where women+ connect to explore, practice, and shape the future of AI together — across Seattle, Portland, Vancouver BC, and remotely.

For women+ who build, use, teach, or are transitioning into AI: ML engineers, data scientists, product managers, designers, founders, students, educators, and researchers.

Community guidelines

Our purpose

Create a safe, supportive learning space for women+.

What we expect

Respect for all participants, consent before sharing others' stories or taking photos, and constructive discussion.

What we don't allow

Harassment, hate speech, intimidation, recruiting spam, or sales pitches.

Privacy

No recording. What's shared here stays here.

Need to report something?

Use the registration form or email us directly. We respond within 48 hours.
